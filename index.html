> About me
I am currently leading the computational team at Allcyte, a precision medicine startup based in Vienna. Using high-throughput microscopy and machine learning, we are developing and deploying functional testing of cancer drug efficacy in primary patient samples.

During my PhD I studied the biology of ageing, analysing and interpreting multi-omics data to investigate the tissue-specific insulin response in mutant flies. I have also worked with sequencing data from a large mouse stem cell screen, teasing apart regulatory networks of self-renewal.

> Web apps using R Shiny
This server was set up to tinker with the shiny web framework for R. Since I'm busy with internal projects, I am not actively updating these any more, but here are two smaller apps I developed while learning:

[CellPlot] generates segmented bar charts. These visualize functional term enrichments (e.g. from the gene ontology analysis of a gene set), and simultaneously the relative changes of individual genes between experimental conditions.

[shinySETGO] determines if a geneset of interest is enriched in known biological functions (gene ontology terms). Redundancy is reduced by clustering terms that contain the same subsets of genes. I wrote the underlying script to simplify these standard analyses in my own projects. Implementing the web app served as a refresher in mySQL.
